BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
BUDT748 is a Capstone class, designed as an industry sponsored practicum project.The course provides students an opportunity to work on a real-world project where they will work with a sponsoring firm to use information technology to solve a real-world business problem. The capstone will develop student skills in interacting with real-world business-technology stakeholders, navigating technology organizations, and presenting technology-based solutions to sponsoring firms.
Semester Details: Fall 2025
